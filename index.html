Diagnostic software for where diagnostics don't reach.

The tools exist. They just don't arrive. Clinoble builds AI-enabled screening and care-linkage software for the sub-centre, the primary health centre, and the rural clinic — the places where a machine, a specialist, or a laboratory was never going to arrive.

Founded and led by a public health physician. Built for the health system that actually exists, not the one in the brochure.

A screening tool is only as good as the threshold you deploy it at — and the cascade you attach it to.

Platforms

Deployed software, not slideware. Each platform is bilingual, offline-capable where it needs to be, and designed to run on the hardware a frontline health worker already carries.

TBFirst

Live

AI-enabled tuberculosis screening and care-linkage. Symptom risk scoring, cough acoustic analysis, and explainable chest-radiograph interpretation — then referral, treatment support, and follow-up tracking, so a positive screen actually becomes a treated patient.

English / తెలుగు · tbfirst.com

TeleVaidyam

Pilot deployment

Rural teleconsultation built for the last mile. Structured AI intake, a 257-molecule formulary, prescriptions explained aloud in the patient's own language, ASHA-assisted booking, and referral into the primary health centre when it matters.

English / తెలుగు · Teleconsultation

CardioPulmo

Research validation

Cardiopulmonary screening from signals a clinic already produces — digital auscultation and chest radiography. Grad-CAM explainability throughout, so every output can be audited by the clinician who has to act on it.

Internal validation · Not for diagnostic use

NETHRA

Live

District-level public health surveillance across all 33 districts of Telangana. Mapping, trend detection, and analytical tooling for the people who have to make the call.

Public health surveillance · Telangana

Decades

Google Play

Biological age and healthspan assessment. Validated phenotypic-age algorithms and functional measures, translated into something a person can actually act on.

Android · Preventive health

PAUSE

Google Play

Digital wellbeing assessment built on published, validated psychometric instruments rather than invented scales. Screening, not diagnosis — and it says so.

Android · Behavioural health
